Body Dysmorphia & Gym Culture

A data science study exploring the mental impact of gym environments and fitness media.

This research project investigates the relationship between gym habits, social media exposure, and body image concerns. Survey responses are analyzed using Python to uncover patterns and statistical insights.

Survey Design

This anonymous survey was distributed via Instagram, Reddit, and Discord, collecting over 40 responses on gym frequency, body image, and media exposure. Questions included Likert scale items, body satisfaction ratings, and PED usage.

Data Analysis (Coming Soon)

Once data collection is complete, I’ll be conducting statistical tests (ANOVA, correlation, regression) and visualizations to better understand how habits and culture influence body dysmorphia.

Early Insights

Initial trends suggest strong links between frequent physique comparison and low body satisfaction. More detailed visualizations and modeling will be added here soon.
